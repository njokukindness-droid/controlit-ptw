How it works

From request to job site
in four stages

CONTROLIT enforces a structured, auditable permit lifecycle that mirrors international PTW standards.

1
Requesting Party
Team leader and supervisor jointly complete and submit the permit form with all job details and hazard information.
2
Area Authority
Confirms area readiness, checks for SIMOPS conflicts, and verifies all isolations are in place before approving.
3
Safety Officer
Reviews the risk assessment, PPE requirements, gas tests, and HSE compliance before forwarding for issuance.
4
Issuer
Goes to site, verifies all controls physically via checklist, signs digitally, and issues the permit. Work may now commence.
